Social Media Marketing

Social media is a great way to reach new customers while re-engaging with existing ones. But only if it’s done effectively! Otherwise, you are throwing money into a void with a random chance of seeing results.With our knowledge and expertise we can help you create (more) effective social ads.

Pixel Tracking Setup

Tracking the behavior of your customers gives valuable insight into their purchase process. Target the right audience at the right place with the right approach. Social Media is much deeper than it seems, and we go in-depth with these and more. We will set up pixel tracking across your channels and measure the quality of the results, making adjustments as needed.

Content Creation

For every social media network we will create unique Content. Because, what works for one network doesn’t necessarily work on others. We at Envision believes in writing witty and concise copy that conveys the right message with an eye-catchy design.

Audience Segmentation

Advertisements are a waste of money if they are shown to the wrong people. Therefore, we will segment your audience to ensure the right ads are displayed to the right people.

Testing & Optimization

Our work is never done. Once we have implemented a paid social strategy, we will test the advertisements and continue to make changes in order to optimize results.

OUR PROCESS

Tracking results, analysing data and then tweaking to optimise them is an art that only keen marketers have. It’s important to make your presence relatable hence optimise your strategy as tour continue to grow and learn more about your business and audience.

01 | Map the Customer Journey

First we begin we the installation of all the trackers, to ensure we can measure the advertising results effectively. Then we start with the audience segmentation.

02 | Create & Test Ads

Secondly, we will create various advertisements, testing with several messages, images, and targeting audiences. Once the ads are launched we continue optimizing them until we find the best combination.

03 | Results & Growth

The optimizations we make will improve your social results. You will see an increase in your social media following, brand awareness, and conversions. This results in making more money.
